Welcome To The Old Skool Skate Shop


About Us



Old Skool Skate Shop is located in Uptown Westervile in the side street across from Greaters Ice Cream.

We have been serving the local skateboarding community since 2001, and host many contests each year. Old Skool is owned and operated by Wally Carl. Wally has been skating since 1986.
